    Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge

    Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge is a river-crossing channel connecting Hanyang District and Wuchang District in Wuhan City, Hubei Province, China. Located on the Yangtze River waterway, it is the first dual-purpose Yangtze River Bridge built after the founding of New China. It is also one of the important historic landmark buildings in Wuhan City and is known as the "No.1 Bridge in Wanli Yangtze River".
