    Sun Yat-sen's Mausoleum

    Sun Yat-sen's Mausoleum is located in Zhongshan Scenic Area, south of Zijin Mountain, Xuanwu District, Nanjing. It is the tomb of Mr. Sun Yat-sen, the pioneer of the great democratic revolution in modern China, and its commemorative architectural complex, covering an area of more than 80,000 square meters.

    Agreement of Nanjing

    The "Nanjing Treaty", or the "Jiang Ning Treaty", is a treaty on reconciliation between China and Britain on the Opium War on August 29, 1842 in Nanjing.

    Nanjing University

    Nanjing University (Nanjing University), referred to as "Nanjing University", is a comprehensive national key university directly under the Ministry of Education of the People's Republic of China and directly under the management of the Central Government.

    Nanjing Massacre Memorial Museum

    The Nanjing Massacre Memorial Hall (commonly known as the Jiangdongmen Memorial) is one of the three internationally recognized memorials of the three tragedies during World War II, a national first-class museum, a national patriotic education demonstration base, a national key cultural relics protection unit, and the first batch of Chinese 20 Century Architectural Heritage List.
