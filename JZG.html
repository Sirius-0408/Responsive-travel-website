World heritage Scenic spots
Jiuzhaigou is located in Jiuzhaigou County, Aba Tibetan and Qiang Autonomous Prefecture, Sichuan Province. It is a valley with a depth of more than 50 kilometers. Jiuzhaigou is named after nine Tibetan villages such as Shuzheng Stockaded Village, Heye Village and Zechawa Village are located in this mountain lake group. Jiuzhaigou World Natural Heritage, National Key Scenic Spots, National AAAAA Tourist Attractions, National Nature Reserve, National Geopark, World Biosphere Reserve Network, is also the first nature reserve in China with the main purpose of protecting natural scenery. It is reputed as "fairy tale world" and "king of waterscape". Scenic spot introduction

History

For thousands of years, Jiuzhaigou has been hidden in the mountains on the northwest plateau of Sichuan, and human activities seem insignificant. Tibetans in this area are almost isolated from the world and live a self-sufficient farming and pastoral life. At present, the earliest relevant records are only Nanping County Annals compiled in the early years of the Republic of China. Jiuzhaigou is named after nine Tibetan villages that have lived here for generations: Shuzheng Stockaded Village, Zechawa, Heijiao, Lotus Leaf, Panya, Yala, Jianpan, Rexi and Guodu.

Geographical environment

①Geographical position

Located in Zhangza Town, Jiuzhaigou County, Aba Tibetan and Qiang Autonomous Prefecture, south of Minshan Mountains in northwest Sichuan Province, it is more than 400 kilometers away from Chengdu.

②Geological landform

Jiuzhaigou is located in the transition zone from Qinghai-Tibet Plateau to Sichuan Basin. The crust has been uplifted greatly and a variety of tectonic forces have been compounded alternately, creating a variety of landforms. The plant karst travertine deposits are the dominant factor, and large-scale karst travertine deposits have developed.

③physioclimate

The plateau has a humid climate with obvious temperature difference in spring, rapid and stable temperature rise in summer, pleasant climate in autumn and colder winter. Rainfall is rare and concentrated throughout the year. July-August is a typical rainy season.

④natural resource

Jiuzhaigou is China's first nature reserve with the main purpose of protecting natural scenery. Its forest coverage exceeds 80%, with a total of 2,000 plants and 18 national protected animals.

Jiuzhaigou has six unique features

Hide one's feelings

Jiuzhaigou has long been a Tibetan settlement. Tibetan culture is mysterious and dignified with distinctive features. Every year in June of the solar calendar, a 5-day "Japanese mulberry cultural festival" is held, in which tourists can participate and watch a series of competitive competitions, such as Tibetan military chess, walking on stilts, folk songs, Tibetan songs and dances, etc. Every year from March to April, there will also be a month of ethnic customs, during which you can observe the architectural culture of ancient Tibetan villages in Wawa and experience Tibetan folk customs, costumes and arts.

Cai Lin

Colorful forests cover more than half the area of the scenic spot, with more than 2,000 kinds of plants. They are colorful and compete with each other. The forest floor was covered with thick moss and littered with feathers of birds and animals. Every autumn, from the bottom of the valley to the top of the mountain, there are thousands of leaves and trees with various colors. Although red, green and yellow are the main colors, their shades are different. For example, natural giant oil paintings are full of primitive forest features.

Cascade waterfalls

Almost all the waterfalls in Jiuzhaigou run out of the dense forest like a loom that never stops. The three most representative waterfalls are Pearl Beach Waterfall, which is about 200 meters wide and has great momentum. It is the scene of the 86-edition classic TV series Journey to the West where Tang Priest and his disciples lead horses and wade through the water. Shuzheng Waterfall is 25 meters high and 72 meters wide at the top. It is formed by several small waterfalls. Nuorilang Waterfall is the widest travertine waterfall in China.

Every winter, Jiuzhaigou becomes especially quiet. Wei Xuefeng, handsome tall and straight, white snow, the scenery is extremely magnificent; The valleys and gullies are faint and have a clear view. festival activities

Tibetan Qiang Style Party

Jiuzhaigou Song and Dance Party mainly includes Tibetan riddles, Jiuzhaigou thousand-year-old feelings, impression of Jiuzhaigou Gao Yuanhong, Tibetan king banquet dance, etc. It shows the folk customs of Tibetan and Qiang peoples in a panoramic manner. < span > through the theatre performers' wonderful interpretation and live interpretation of traditional dances, music, props and instrumental music, the audience's emotions can be brought into it < /span>, adding extraordinary splendor to Jiuzhaigou.

Ice Falls Festival

November 16 to March 31 of the following year is Jiuzhaigou's annual winter tour time, and Jiuzhaigou's biggest winter festival, Ice Falls Festival, is also officially celebrated. < span > The highlight of the Ice Waterfall Festival is Lan Bing, one of the "Nine Strongholds and Six Wonders". Icicles of different shapes are radiant with faint blue light in the sun, just like exotic gems. Not only that, during the Ice Waterfall Festival, you can also experience the exotic customs of the Tibetan and Qiang ethnic groups, the charming folk lion dances and mountain turns.

Folk Custom Month

Jiuzhaigou Folk Custom Month is held in March and April of each year for a period of one month to show Jiuzhaigou's remote ethnic culture and charming folk customs. < span > Jiuzhaigou folk customs month mainly focuses on beautiful scenery, delicious food, costumes, wedding exhibitions, etc., accompanied by rich local characteristics of folk culture performances, and will also hold activities such as amorous feelings carnival night. < /span> during the folk customs month, tourists at home and abroad can not only enjoy the folk arts and dances with a long history and unique characteristics, the national intangible cultural heritage, namely, the humble dance, listen to the broad folk songs that naturally have a strong flavor of national life, but also observe the architectural culture of the ancient Tibetan village of wa.

Manzhi Culture Festival

The Zharu Temple in Jiuzhaigou holds ritual activities such as sacrifice and praying for blessings in the Muzhi Festival every year on the lunar calendar. The monks of the temple prepare for the ritual activities more than one month in advance. Starting from the 8th day of the fourth month of the lunar calendar, the monks in the temple chant Buddhist scripture every day until the 14th day of the fourth month of the lunar calendar and the 15th day of the fourth month of the lunar calendar.

Main Activities: Three days before the Maze meeting, the lamas of the whole temple will focus on the monastery to eat vegetarian food, chant Buddhist scripture and rehearse the program. On the day of the temple fair, people in hundreds of miles around dressed in magnificent festival costumes, holding dragons, incense candles, holding Thangka, and praying with full devotion to Zharu temple.

Japanese mulberry cultural festival

Japanese-mulberry cultural festival is also called the festival of offering "holy mountain and holy water". Jiuzhaigou Tibetans believe in Bon Religion, and Nissan Cultural Festival is a traditional festival for them to worship the sacred mountain and water. Jiuzhaigou is held once a year in April (June in the Gregorian calendar) for five days. < span > during Jiuzhaigou Nissan cultural festival, tents will be set up in an open area with a "lecture main tent" in the middle, which is specially prepared for eminent monks who lecture and talk about the scriptures. The surrounding area is a group of village tents for Tibetan compatriots from nine Tibetan villages in Jiuzhaigou. < /span> during the Japanese-mulberry cultural festival, people in the villages of the scenic spot will have competitions such as carrying water, holding highland barley bags, setting up grain, Guo te (walking on stilts), Tibetan military chess and mother tongue narrative competitions. There are also various traditional folk songs, dances and other performances.
