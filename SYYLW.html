Yalong Bay, Sanya, Hainan
4A scenic spot
Yalong Bay is a high-quality tropical seaside scenic spot in the eastern suburb of Sanya City in Hainan Province, known as "Oriental Hawaii". It is a national 4A-class tourist scenic spot, which integrates the five major elements of modern tourism: ocean, beach, sunshine, green and fresh air, presenting an obvious tropical marine climate. Yalong Bay has an eight kilometer long silvery white beach with fine sand, clean and transparent sea water and lush trees on the shore. The annual average temperature here is 25.5℃ and the water temperature is 20℃. It is an ideal winter retreat and leisure resort.

Yalong Bay integrates the five elements of modern tourism: ocean, sand beach, sunshine, green and fresh air, presenting an obvious tropical marine climate. Butterfly Valley

Butterfly Valley is located at the back of Yalong Bay Little longtan lake. It is not far from the central square. It is surrounded by mountains on both sides. The middle valley bottom runs through a stream and covers an area of 1.5 hectares. The valley has a large ecological butterfly garden and a museum with more than 2,000 precious butterfly and insect specimens. < span > there are more than 500 kinds of exquisite butterfly and insect specimens on display in the specimen exhibition hall. among them, there are precious pteridopterus aureomaculatus, pteridopterus trifasciatus, pteridopterus trifasciatus, pteridopterus multifida, apollo silk butterfly, tortoises with four spots, tortoises with long arms, tortoises with fork rhinoceros, horsetail bees and world representative butterflies. The specimen making room is a workplace for making butterfly specimens and handicrafts. The natural dead butterflies are recycled and processed, thus realizing the reuse of resources.

Yalong Bay Forest Park

Yalong Bay Tropical Forest Park is located 25 kilometers southeast of Sanya, the southernmost international tropical coastal tourist city in China. It is the mountain on both sides of Yalong Bay National Tourist Resort, with a total area of 1506 hectares. It is divided into East Park and West Park, just like stretching arms around "the first bay in the world". The vegetation types are tropical evergreen rainforest and tropical semi-deciduous monsoon forest. Its abundant biological, geographical, astronomical, hydrological, human and seascape resources and unique location advantages make it a tourist attraction for tropical rainforest. She and Yalong Bay National Tourism Resort have formed strong differences, complementation and interaction. It is an important extension of Yalong Bay from coast to mountain, from ocean to forest, from plane to stereo, and from blue to green. It has really formed the concept of large scenic spot in Yalong Bay and filled the gap of forest eco-tourism in Sanya.

Forest plant landscape

The forest vegetation types of Yalong Bay Tropical Forest Park are tropical evergreen rainforest and tropical semi-deciduous monsoon forest. It is rich in diversity, with more than 1,500 species belonging to 133 families. Forest structure is complex and seasonal changes are colorful.

Hongxia Ridge, the main peak, Zhuluo Ridge, Feilong Ridge, Qingyun Ridge and Longtousling Ridge have special light, heat and rain conditions at an altitude of 250 meters above sea level and have developed into good tropical evergreen rainforests. The main tree species are banyan, sycamore, white tea, closed flowers and trees, rattan bamboo, pine, myrtle, etc. Among them, the tall banyan tree, which often hugs boulders and is superior to the others, has a large trunk and can be folded by several people. Its branches extend and its crown is huge. It can be called the "welcome banyan tree" in the garden. The beautiful phoenix tree, like her name, is a beautiful landscape tree in the mountain, which blooms every May. Another kind of plant should be rattan bamboo, which creeps on the ground and climbs in the forest like rattan. The strange thing is that bamboo shoots do not grow in the ground but on bamboo poles. Perhaps this is how the "cutting off the branches" came about. This rainforest is full of branches and leaves, with various forest types. Its characteristics such as root, strangulation and parasitism are obvious. All species are distributed in three-dimensional and multi-layer and blend with each other. It has the characteristics of Hainan tropical rainforest. < span > Tropical semi-deciduous monsoon forest is distributed in areas below 250 meters above sea level. The main tree species are Cycas hainanensis, Prunus mume, Dalbergia hainanensis, Hedysarum microphylla, Dendrocalamus lingnanensis, Castanopsis hainanensis, Acacia pinnatifida, Elaeagnus angustifolia, etc. Among them, Cycas hainanensis is the national first-class protected tree species, while Prunus mume and Dalbergia odorifera are the national second-class protected tree species.

Geographical landscape

"The mountain is not high, but a fairy is famous". Hongxia Ridge, the main peak, is 450 meters above sea level. There is a group of natural boulders on the summit. The overall image resembles a giant Buddha Maitreya sitting with his hands folded and his eyes closed. A big banyan tree with a crooked neck in his arms is like a leading crutch, lifelike. Local villagers have worshipped the stone as a mountain god since ancient times. This landscape has a good visual effect in the south half of the main peak and even along the seashore. Here, looking up at the sky and overlooking the sea, the pleasure of seeing the sea and the wind is coming. This strange stone landscape is the commander-in-chief attraction of the main peak of the park. In addition to the main peak, there are Weifeng Ridge, Zhuluo Ridge, Feilong Ridge, Longtou Ridge, Qingmei Ridge, Shuanglong Ridge, etc. In addition to the undulating mountains in the park, there are outstanding rocks in the mountain. The famous rocks include sun stone, Feilai stone, wild elephant stone, hippopotamus stone, pig hoof stone, white tiger stone, immortal foot stone, sheep head stone, turtle stone, running water stone, etc. They are both cliffs and cliffs, and also have deep and steep valleys and streams. geographic environment

Position

Yalong Bay, located 28 kilometers southeast of Sanya City, is a half-moon bay at the southernmost tip of Hainan, with a total length of about 7.5 kilometers. It is one of the famous sights in Hainan.

Climate

Yalong Bay has an obvious tropical marine monsoon climate, with the annual average temperature of 25.5℃, and the soft and delicate beaches stretch for about 8 kilometers. The visibility of seawater is 7-9 meters, the submarine coral reefs are well preserved, and there are many tropical fish species with different forms and colorful colors, belonging to the national key coral reef protection area. The lowest temperature of seawater in winter is 22 degrees, which is suitable for swimming in all seasons and carrying out all kinds of sea sports. The bay here covers an area of 66 square kilometers and can accommodate 100,000 people to swim in the water and thousands of yachts to swim and chase.
