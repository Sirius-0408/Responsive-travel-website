The main landscape of Potala Palace consists of palaces, murals and spiritual pagodas. scenic spot introduction

Historical culture

Potala Palace was built in the 1930s. It has a history of 1,300 years and was built by the 33rd generation of Tubo Zampsongzangampo. The basic features of the Potala Palace are mainly the White House rebuilt in Gushi Khan and the Fifth Dalai Lama in the 17th century A.D. and the Red Palace built after their death.

Famous figures: Princess Wencheng and Songzan Ganbu

Geographical environment

Potala Palace, located on Mount Maburi northwest of Lhasa, capital of Tibet Autonomous Region of China, is 3700 meters above sea level. The weather in Lhasa throughout the year is mainly sunny, with less precipitation. The annual precipitation is about 500mm. It is reputed as "Sunshine City". The semi-arid climate of typical plateau monsoon is affected by downward flow all year round, and the summer temperature is not high. At this time, the temperature in Lhasa is about 25 -30 degrees, and the highest temperature is only 30 degrees, but the winter is cold and dry, with the lowest temperature reaching-16.5 degrees.

Scenic spot transportation

featured landscape

Palace

The palace buildings are built one after another, twists and turns, and organically merge with the mountain. This is the direct experience of Potala Palace. The palace of Potala Palace was designed and built according to the rule of sunlight in the plateau area. The palace also houses Tibetan-specific Thangka painted on cotton satin and historical relics.

Famous scenic spots: pagbalha Kang-Saint Guanyin Hall, Tsochinszepine Tsoonu-West Hall, its Virtue Danji-Changshoule Hall, Dukuokang-Shilun Hall, etc.

Mural

A large number of murals are painted inside Potala Palace, forming a huge painting gallery. Nearly 200 people took part in the mural painting successively, which took more than 10 years. The murals cover the history of Tibetan Buddhism, the life of Lama Shida, the process of Princess Wencheng's entry into Tibet, ancient Tibetan architectural images and a large number of Buddha statues. King Kong is a precious historical seal script.

Venue: On the walls of all palaces, Buddhist temples and corridors of Potala Palace

Treasure collection

The Potala Palace Treasure Museum displays about 200 pieces of palace cultural relics, including letters patent, seals, gifts, literature and ancient books, Beiye sutra, Buddha statues, Tangka, artifacts, etc., mainly including the Ming and Qing dynasties' legal relics, rare Tibetan classics, and rare cultural relics in deep hiding.

Venue: Treasure Museum of Potala Palace

Statue

The statue of Potala Palace is also a special landscape. There are tens of thousands of statues in Potala Palace, which are distributed in various palaces. Apart from the major Buddhist statues, it is famous for Songzan Ganbu Statue and Wencheng Princess Statue.

Venue: palaces

Pagoda

The spiritual pagoda is the place where the remains of the Dalai Lama were laid down. from the 5th to the 13th, except for the six different worlds where they were dismissed from their teaching posts, the other eight built luxurious spiritual pagodas. These spiritual pagodas are different in size, but in the same form. They all consist of a tower top, a tower bottle and a tower base.

Venue: Wuzijiapi-Jinding Group

scenic spot map

pagbalha Kang-San Guan Yin Dian

The Kang-Sheng Guanyin Temple in pagbalha is the main Buddha temple for Potala Palace, and the temple is the main temple for the holy Guanyin image, a valuable cultural relic of the town palace of Potala Palace. In addition, there are more than 100 kinds of statues, pagodas and other precious cultural relics in the temple, which is an ancient and sacred temple in Potala Palace.

The Fifth Dalai Lama's Temple of Spiritual Pagoda

Located on the west side of the west hall of the red palace, the hall of the fifth Dalai Lama's spiritual tower was built between 1690 and 1693 a.d. it has a four-storey appearance and is one of the famous halls of the Potala palace. In the temple, there are gold pagodas dedicated to the 5th, 10th and 12th Dalai Lama and eight silver pagodas dedicated to the deceased. In addition to offering more than 1640 relief offerings of silver and gold gilt, the pagoda and the pagoda are in front of them.

Qujiezhupu-the cave of the French king

Located on the north side of the 6th floor of the Red Palace, it is one of the earliest buildings in Potala Palace, which has a history of more than 1300 years. In the cave of the French king are clay figurines of Tubo zanpu songzangampo, Tang wencheng princess, Nepalese chizun princess, Tubo minister tummie sambuza and lotung zan, prince gong zigong zan and others.

Syracuse

Syracuse refers to all the buildings on the front of the Potala Palace. Syracuse is an important part of the Potala Palace complex. Its history is as long as that of Potala Palace. At present, it is open to most of the public in the form of original display, restoration display, dynamic display and special exhibition, while the Potala Palace Treasure Museum and tourist service center are set up.

BuDaLaGong ZhenBaoGuan

Treasure Hall is the place where the Potala Palace displays cultural relics. Its cultural relics cover Tibet's history, culture, art and other aspects. The Potala Palace Treasure Hall, which has more than 200 rare treasures, has officially opened its arms to the world, becoming a window for people to understand and understand Tibet's history and culture, and will become another fascinating secret on the snowy plateau.

Deyangxia-East Courtyard

Deyangxia-East Courtyard is located on the east side of the White House and covers an area of over 1300 square meters. It is the venue for large-scale performances and other activities in the Potala Palace in old Tibet. At present, the eastern courtyard and its surrounding monastic schools and government houses are set aside as office buildings for the Potala palace administration.

Tsochinszepine Tsoonu-West Hall

Tsochin Siping Tsonu West Hall, located in the center of the fourth floor of the Red Palace, covers an area of 776.2 square meters and is the hall for the 5th Dalai Lama. The walls around the temple are painted with 2215 groups of murals, recording the family history of the 5th Dalai Lama and his biography. Among them, about 15 groups of murals in the northeast corner tell the whole process of the Fifth Dalai Lama's visit to Beijing in 1652 AD to meet the Emperor Shunzhi of the Qing Dynasty, which is especially precious.

Wuzi Jiapi-Jinding Group

Jinding Group is a unique landscape of Potala Palace. It is located at the top of the Red Palace, the main building of Potala Palace at an altitude of more than 3,750 meters. There are 7 Jinding Groups, all made of copper and gold. They are the Jinding of the Lingta Hall of Dalai Lama and the Jinding of the Buddha Hall of Potala Palace. When you climb on the Jinding Group, you can see blue sky and white clouds when looking up, and you can watch the general picture of the ancient city when looking over the railing. You can take pictures of your unforgettable moment here, which can reflect the characteristics of Tibet.

Dukuo Lacan-Houlun Hall

The shilun temple is located on the east side of the sixth floor of the red palace, covering an area of 108.57 square meters. In the center of the temple is a bronze and gold-plated hour-wheel stereo altar city. The surrounding shrines are dedicated to 168 statues of famous calendar inheritors from India, mainland China, Tibet and other places, one bronze and gold-plated hour-wheel pagoda, eight silver pagodas, 38 hour-wheel statues and other precious statues.

Its Virtue Danji-Longevity Music Hall

The temple is located in the southwest corner of the highest level of the red palace, with an area of 246.17 square meters. it used to be the bedroom of the 6th Dalai Lama, cangyangjiacuo. the murals depicted in the temple "asked by the disciple of the founder of kagyang" are very precious and worthy of appreciation. In addition, the Hall of Longevity Music also honors 1,000 boundless longevity Buddhas and over 100 Buddha statues of various types.

Senjoni Weinu-West Sunlight Hall

The West Sunlight Hall is located in the west of the top floor of the White House and faces south. It has a large lighting area and is sunny from morning till night. Therefore, it is called the West Sunlight Hall. The exquisite furnishings, antiques and jewelry, national handicrafts and statues of Tang Ga in the temple dazzle people. Stepping out of the bedroom, there is a flat balcony outside, overlooking by railing, giving a panoramic view of Lhasa.

Songgotha-White House Door

This is the entrance to the front of Potala Palace. The entrance hall can be reached by climbing twelve stone steps to three wooden ladders (the middle ladder is dedicated to the Dalai Lama). The gate is square, with four pillars and eight beams inside. Lotus flowers and eight pharmacists are carved between the pillars. Tiger, lion, Peng and dragon are carved in the center of the sparrow bank. The surrounding walls are painted with exquisite murals.

practical information

Scenic weather

Lhasa City is located in the northern side of the Himalayas. Influenced by downward flow, it has sunny weather throughout the year, with little rainfall, no severe cold in winter and no severe heat in summer. It is a plateau temperate semi-arid monsoon climate. The highest temperature in history is 29.6 degrees Celsius, the lowest temperature is minus 16.5 degrees Celsius, and the annual average temperature is 7.4 degrees Celsius. Rainfall is concentrated in June, July, August and September, and sunshine lasts more than 3,000 hours throughout the year. It is known as the "Sunshine City".

Warm reminder

dressing guide

(1) Lhasa is in a plateau climate with a low average temperature and a large temperature difference between morning and evening. Therefore, attention should be paid to keeping warm when going to Lhasa. In terms of dress, casual clothes are the main style, and double-layer and thickened coats are selected.

(2) Lhasa has strong solar radiation and long sunshine hours. Attention should be paid to sun protection measures and sun protection cream, sunglasses, hats, etc.

traffic guide

External transportation of scenic spots

The Potala Palace is located in Lhasa, the capital of Tibet, so the transportation is very convenient. There are many kinds of transportation options to Lhasa: by plane, by train, by high-speed passenger or by self-driving.

Aircraft

Lhasa Gongga Airport-The airport has special civil aviation buses, which can be seen from 06:30 to 19:30 at intervals of about half an hour to one hour. The bus fare is in 25 yuan and the terminal is the Civil Aviation Administration next to Potala Palace. The journey is 67 kilometers and the time is about one hour. Taxi is probably in 150 yuan, so you need to make an appointment in advance.

Railway station

Lhasa Station-Lhasa Station is 5.4km away from Potala Palace. Bus No.89 can be taken from Lhasa Station to Potala Palace. The third stop is Potala Palace Square. The fare is 1 yuan. However, please pay attention to the time. Bus No.89 is collected at 8pm. You can also take buses No.1, No.13 and No.14. The fare is one yuan and the drive is about 40 minutes. In addition, you can take a taxi directly. The fare is about 26 minutes in 20 yuan.

Bus station

There are mainly four bus terminals in Lhasa, namely, the eastern suburbs, the northern suburbs, the western suburbs and Liuwu bus terminals. Each bus terminal undertakes different transportation directions.

The following contents of the eastern suburb passenger station: mainly buses to Linzhi, Bayi town and other places.

The following is the content of the passenger station in the northern suburbs: mainly buses to Ali, Naqu and other places.

The following is the content of Xijiao Passenger Station: It is mainly for buses to Xigaze, Shannan and Naqu.

Liu Wu bus station has the following contents: it is mainly for buses to Changdu, Zhangmu and other places.

After arriving at the passenger station, transfer to a bus. These bus stations all have buses that directly reach Potala Palace, with a fare of one yuan, or take a taxi, with a price within 20 yuan.

Self - driving

If you drive to Lhasa's Potala Palace, you can choose Sichuan-Tibet line and Qinghai-Tibet line.

Sichuan-Tibet line

The Sichuan-Tibet line is the route with the most beautiful scenery and the lowest average altitude, with good road conditions. There is a direct highway from Chengdu to Lhasa, along which you can also go to scenic spots such as Aden and Shangri-La in Daocheng. The Sichuan-Tibet line is suitable for tourists from Sichuan, Chongqing and other places who want to drive to Lhasa's Potala Palace.

Qingzang line

Tourists from Gansu and Qinghai can choose the Qinghai-Tibet line to go to Lhasa. The scenery of the Qinghai-Tibet line is relatively simple, the average altitude is high, and the Sichuan-Tibet line is prone to traffic jams. However, the landscape along the way is quite majestic and rich, passing through Kunlun mountain pass, Tanggula mountain pass, various Tibetan antelope protection stations, and crossing Hoh Xil. Can see grasslands, salt lakes, Gobi, mountains, deserts and other landscapes.
