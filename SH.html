Shanghai

City business card

Shanghai (abbreviation: Shanghai, Shanghai) is China's national central city, the core city of the Shanghai-Hangzhou-Ningbo Greater Bay Area, the international economic, financial, trade, shipping, and technological innovation center, and is the first batch of coastal open cities. Shanghai is located at the mouth of the Yangtze River. It is a leading city in the Yangtze River Economic Belt.
