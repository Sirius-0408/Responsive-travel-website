scenic spot introduction

Historical culture

Huangshan, formerly known as Mount Fu, is named for its black peaks and blue rocks, and its distant view of Cang Dai. Legend has it that Yellow Emperor Xuanyuan used to gather herbs and practice alchemy here to become immortal. Emperor Xuanzong of the Tang Dynasty believed in Taoism, and in the 6th year of Tianbao (747 A.D.), he changed Mount Fu to Mount Huangshan. The name of Mount Huangshan has been used ever since. For more than 1,000 years, Huangshan has accumulated a rich culture of the Yellow Emperor. The famous scenic spots of Xuanyuan Peak, Alchemy Peak, Rongcheng Peak, Fuqiu Peak, Danjing, Xiyaoxi and Shayaotai are all related to the Yellow Emperor.

Huangshan is the mountain of culture. Huangshan's cultural resources can be summarized by "five wins", that is, historical relics, calligraphy and painting, literature, legends and celebrities.

Famous celebrities in Huangshan Scenic Spot: Li Bai, Zhao Kuangyin, Zhang Daqian, Zhang Xueliang, Zhou Enlai, Liu Bocheng, Deng Xiaoping, Mark Lieb, Jiang Zemin, etc.

Geographical environment

Huangshan is located in Huangshan City in the south of Anhui Province, with a length of about 40 kilometers from north to south, a width of about 30 kilometers from east to west, and a total area of about 1200 square kilometers. Among them, Huangshan Scenic Area covers an area of 160.6 square kilometers, stretching from Yellow Lion in the east to Xiaolingjiao in the west, Erlong Bridge in the north and Tangkou Town in the south. It is divided into nine management areas: hot spring, Yungu, Yuping, Beihai, Songgu, Diaoqiao, Fuxi, Yanghu and Fugu.

Huangshan has a geological history of 800 million years. It integrates peak forest landform and glacier relics, and has rich and typical geological landscapes such as granite sculpted stones, granite caverns and Quantan Creek Waterfall. The Qianshan rock mass is sparsely jointed, multi-spherical weathered, and the mountain is vigorous and spectacular. The rock mass in the back mountain is densely jointed, with many columns weathered, and the mountain is steep, forming the geomorphological feature of "majestic front mountain and beautiful back mountain". In 2004, it was selected as "National Geopark".

Huangshan is located in a subtropical monsoon climate zone, with no severe summer heat in summer and less severe winter. The average temperature difference in the four seasons is only about 20 degrees Celsius. The scenic area is densely forested, with numerous streams and waterfalls. The air quality maintains Grade I all the year round. The daily average concentration of air PM2.5 is 5 micrograms/cubic meter. The concentration of air negative oxygen ions is stable at 20,000 /cm3   all the year round. Above, it is called "natural oxygen bar".

featured landscape

Huangshan represents the landscape with "five unique features and three waterfalls". The five unique features are Qisong, strange stone, sea of clouds, hot spring and winter snow. The three waterfalls are herringbone waterfall, baizhang spring and Jiulong waterfall.

Qisong

Huangshan pine is a variety of Chinese pine trees formed by the unique landform and climate of Huangshan. Huangshan scenic spot is known as "no tree is not pine, no stone is not pine, no pine is not strange".

Ten famous pines in Huangshan: welcome pine, hope pine, send pine, visit pine, cattail pollen pine, black tiger pine, wolong pine, kylin pine and lianli pine.

Since ancient times, Huang Shanyun has become a sea. Its magnificent and spectacular "sea of clouds" is famous for its beauty, victory, wonder and illusion. Scenic weather

local climate

The climate of Huangshan (mountain) is characterized by wet spring, cool summer, dry autumn and cold winter. At the top of Huangshan Mountain, spring begins in mid-May. The average temperature in July and August is 17-18 degrees. At that time, the average monthly temperature at the foot of the mountain was 24-25 degrees, and there was a great disparity at the foot of the mountain. < span > precipitation is especially concentrated in spring (March-may) and summer (June-August) in Huangshan region. winter begins around the end of September. in the long seven months, snow, ice, frost and sleet alternate.

Plum rains are obvious: Huangshan region usually has plum rains in mid-June and plum rains in mid-July. The plum rains last for about a month, and the amount of plum rains usually accounts for 25%-30% of the annual rainfall, so the humidity in Huangshan is very high at this time. < strong > to travel to Huangshan at this time, you should bring rain gear and proper clothes to prevent rain and humidity. old people and children should avoid traveling to Huangshan at this time. climbing on slippery roads is not safe, so watch the weather forecast in advance.

meteorological landscape

(1) Snow cover: Due to the relatively high snow cover, it often rains below the mountain in winter and snows on the mountain. The early snow day is one and a half months earlier than the lower counties, and the final snow day is about 18 days later.

(3) rime and rime: rime is a natural wonder of winter tourism in Huangshan. Huangshan is mostly grainy rime, forming at-2℃-7℃. When droplets develop into rain in Mao Mao, glaze can form.

(4) Sea of Clouds: Huangshan Sea of Clouds has more momentum and features than any other mountain. The basic features of Huangshan's sea of clouds landscape are magnificent and magical, unpredictable, cohesive and difficult to disperse, and vast.

Starting from September 1, 2018, the real-name booking system will be implemented for tent camping in Huangshan Scenic Area. Those who bring their own tents or rent accommodation tents on the mountain need to make an appointment in advance. The camping tents will be set up from 18: 00 to the early departure of the next day, and the rental tents will cost 50-100 yuan.

There is one center and seven sub-centers providing camping tent service in the scenic spot, namely Beihai, Shilin, Xihai, Paiyun Building, Baiyun, Yuping Building and White Goose. Tourists can freely choose to reserve these seven sub-centers as camping locations.
