Inagi Aden Scenic Area
4A scenic spot Nature conservation
Daocheng Aden is a national 4A-level tourist attraction and a national nature reserve. The scenic spot not only has magnificent and sacred snowy mountains, but also has vast meadows, colorful forests and clear blue and transparent Haizi. Almost all the most beautiful things on the snowy plateau are gathered here. It is reputed as the "soul of Shangri-La" and is one of the best tourist and scientific exploration resorts.

scenic spot introduction

Historical culture

In 1928, American botanist and explorer Joseph Charles Francis Rock arrived here. After returning home, he wrote an article in the US National Geographic magazine and published the photos taken. He introduced Aden to the world and was praised as "the last piece of pure land on the water blue planet" by international friends.

Geographical environment

① geographical location

Shangri-La Town, Daocheng County, Ganzi Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture, Sichuan Province.

② geology and geomorphology

The north is a typical hilly plateau, the middle part of which belongs to the original mountain landform area, and the south is a mountain canyon area. The terrain is complex, with high northwest and low southeast, with rolling mountains and overlapping cockroaches.

③ natural climate

Continental monsoon plateau weather, long winter and short summer, large temperature difference between day and night, with an annual average temperature of 12.3 degrees. The rainy season is July and August. Rainfall predominates in areas below 5000 meters above sea level, while snow or hail predominates in extremely high mountain areas above 5000 meters above sea level.

④ natural resources

The forest area is 560 square kilometers, with 1126 species of vascular plants and 291 species of vertebrates.

featured landscape

three religious lord snow mountain

The famous three sacred mountains in the scenic spot: Yangmaiyong, Xiannairi, and Chenodoji. Yang Maiyong means Manjusri Bodhisattva in Tibetan, ranking first among the three parents. Standing at the foot of the mountain, he can see all the forests, grasslands and streams. Xiannairi, which means Guanyin Bodhisattva, is the northern peak of the three parents' snow mountain. Its mountain looks like a giant Buddha leaning back. Xiannairi is a sacred mountain near the entrance to the scenic spot. Charnodogyi means Bodhisattva Vajra Hand. Seen from the ravine, the snow mountain has a silhouette effect, and rocks and ice and snow are rendered very beautiful.

Best place to watch: Yangmaiyong-Luorong Cattle Farm; Fairy is the day-the sea of pearls; Chenoduji-Chonggu Lawn

Luorong Cattle Farm

Backed by three sacred mountains, it is a thoroughfare leading to the milk sea and the five-color sea, and is a pasture for Tibetan people to graze nearby. The Luorong cattle farm is surrounded by endless alpine meadows. Every spring and summer, wild flowers bloom everywhere one after another, which is very magnificent. The Gongga River passes through the cattle farm. Groups of cattle and sheep graze leisurely on the edge. It is a harmonious scene. Tourists who like hiking and picnicking must not miss it.

Milk sea

One of the holy lakes in Aden scenic area is surrounded by snow-capped mountains and is located in the col below the mt. yangmaiyong. it is an ancient glacial lake, shaped like water drops, with blue snow water in the middle and a milky white circle around it. this milky white circle is roughly the origin of the name milk sea. The lake water in the milk sea is of different depths. The nearshore is slightly black due to the precipitation of plants at the bottom of the lake for a long time. The deeper you look, the bluer and brighter you are. Whenever a breeze blows, the lake shines like a gem.

The Five Colors Sea

Between Shenshan Xiannairi and Yangmaiyong is the highest lake in Aden. Starting from the right side of the milk sea, turn up a steep slope and climb another 100 meters to overlook the five-color sea on the ridge. The lake of the Five Colors Sea is clear and transparent. The bottom of the lake is like a grid drawn with ink pens, filled with thick and light blue pigments. If the sky is clear and the air is clear, the plants at the bottom of the lake and the lake will show different light under the sunshine, but the gradual transition has subtle changes.

ocean of pearls

Pearl Sea is a sea child formed by melting snow from Shenshan Mountain in Xiannai and Ri. It is called "Zhuomalacuo" in Tibetan. Zhuo Ma is transliterated from "Dom" in Tibetan, meaning "fairy". Although the area of this fairy lake is not large, it is like an emerald inlaid on a lotus throne in the dense forest, rippling with blue waves and reflecting a colorful world. It is dazzling and is the best place to shoot the reflection of the fairy mountain.

chonggu temple

Chong Gu Temple has an excellent geographical position in the scenic spot. At its foot there are continuous grasslands, and in the distance there are snow-capped mountains that have not changed for a thousand years. Behind it are canyons. Chong Gu Temple was founded in Yuan Dynasty, nearly 800 years ago, and only after restoration can it be seen today. From a distance, the Chonggu Temple hidden in the colorful foliage forest is very beautiful, as if it is one integrated mass with the world. At present, clay statues of Sakyamuni and Manjusri Bodhisattva are enshrined in the temple. The decoration is a typical Tibetan palace architectural style.

Chonggu meadow

Chong Gu Meadow is the mouth of U-shaped valley of Aden glacier. It is a natural bonsai made up of grassland, forest, stream and mani pile. It is here that the movie "Passing Through Your World" finally proposed. Around the meadow, surrounded by mountains, several snow-capped mountains appear and disappear in the clouds. When spring comes, a new green and a flourishing wild flower scene. Autumn is full of poetry, with strong golden yellow and deep red, which is very suitable for photography.

Hell Canyon of Kasi

The valley is 18 kilometers long. The natural landscape in the valley is similar to the hell valley mentioned in Buddhist books. In the book, it is one of the eight resin forests in the world and the only way for human body to pass from the earth to heaven. The primeval forest in hell valley is luxuriant, with thousands of years of cypress trees, calcified pools and calcified waterfalls, as well as black waterfalls and dolomite weathering mirrors, scales, meat chopping boards, etc. the unique landscape is unforgettable and leads to the three parents' main snow mountain.

Sea of Wisdom

After crossing the Songdu pass on the milk sea, you can find the sea of wisdom by walking along the mountain path. Although the sea of wisdom is not as famous as the sea of milk, it is still worth appreciating. Under the contrast of Yongmeiyong Mountain, the lake looks empty and clear. Every time in the dry season, you can also see the calcified ravines strewn at random at the bottom of the lake, just like an artwork woven from vines.

Frog lake

Frog Lake is the deepest sea in the scenic spot, with an area larger than the sum of milk sea and five-color sea. Every time in spring and summer, the mountains around the lake will open azaleas to surround the frog lake. The lake is blue and green. The azaleas and the magnificence of Mount Sinai are reflected in the lake together. The scenery is very beautiful. If you are lucky, you can also meet frogs scampering in the lake.

travel equipment

Cold medicine, certificates, rain gear, sunscreen, sunglasses, wound dressing ......

Dressing guidelines

The scenic spot has high altitude and low temperature, so you need to carry some warm clothes whenever you want. In addition, due to the altitude, Aden area has strong ultraviolet rays, so you must remember to bring sunscreen, hats, sunglasses and other protective equipment. At the same time, in order to ensure the comfort and safety of the trip, a pair of reliable hiking shoes is essential.

Oxygen inhalation service

It is mainly provided in three places: zhaguanbeng service center, chonggu temple service center and nuorong cattle farm service center. If there is any change, please refer to the actual situation of the scenic spot.

Matters needing attention

(1) The editor of MAIGOO.com has a warm reminder: Aden Scenic Area is located in the plateau and the weather is changeable. Prepare cold-proof clothes, anti-hypoxia drugs (such as nifedipine, aminophylline, etc.), sunscreen and skin care products and rain-proof tools. At the same time, exercise less, drink more and do not drink alcohol.

(2) please don't tease wild animals to prevent bites; Do not smoke in non-smoking areas.

(3) The sightseeing walkway in the scenic spot is a steel grid walkway. Do not wear high heels.
