Mount Tai scenic spot
World heritage World geology 5A scenic spot
Mount Tai is one of the five famous mountains in China. It is a world natural and cultural heritage, a world geological park, a national AAAAA scenic spot, a national key scenic spot and a national civilized scenic spot. Since ancient times, Mount Tai has been regarded as a paradise "straight through the seat of the emperor". It has become a sacred mountain worshipped by the people and offered sacrifices to emperors. There is a saying that "Mount Tai is safe and the whole world is safe." practical information

Historical culture

At the beginning of his reign and at the age of peace, the ancient emperors often came to Mount Tai to hold a ceremony to celebrate the closing of the retreat, offering sacrifices to heaven and earth. During the pre-Qin period, 72 monarchs went to mount tai to seal their retreat. From the Qin and Han Dynasties to the Ming and Qing Dynasties, emperors went to Mount Tai for 27 retreats, such as Qin Shihuang, Tang Xuanzong, Tang Gaozong, Wu Zetian, etc. In addition, the stone carvings and steles on Mount Tai, which are the culmination of Chinese calligraphy, are one of the birthplaces of ancient culture in the Yellow River basin.

Geographical environment

geographic location

Mount Tai runs through the central part of Shandong, within the territory of Tai 'an City, and between Tai 'an City and Ji 'nan City. On the east is the sea and on the west is the Yellow River. The main, branch and residual arteries involve more than ten surrounding counties, with a total area of 242 square kilometers.

geology and geomorphology

Mount Tai is the highest and largest mountain in the hills of Shandong Province. The stratum is the typical basement and caprock structure area of North China Platform. The basement is the Archean Taishan Group stratum, with an absolute age of about 2.5 billion years. It is one of the oldest strata in China.

natural climate

The average temperature at the foot of the mountain is -3℃ in January, the peak is -9℃, the average temperature at the foot of the mountain is 26℃ in July, and the peak is 18℃. Winter extreme top minimum temperature-27.5 c. There are four distinct seasons at the foot of the mountain and spring at the top of the mountain. The annual precipitation increases with the height.

natural resources

1136 species of higher plants have been found, with a vegetation coverage rate of 78%, and 34 species of famous trees over 100 years old. There are 448 kinds of medicinal plants, among which Polygonum multiflorum, Polygonatum sibiricum, Prunus armeniaca and Arnebia euchroma are reputed as the four famous drugs in Mount Tai. There are 11 species of animals, more than 800 species of insects and 140 species of birds. Among the fishes, red scale fish is famous and is endemic to Mount Tai.

featured landscape

Taishan sunrise

Standing on the top of Dai, he lifted up his eyes and looked to the east. The misty fog curtain changed from gray to yellow, and from yellow to orange. The whole sky was red and purple. It changed rapidly. The clouds were in different shapes. The rosy clouds all over the sky merged with the vast sea of clouds on the horizon. It was like a huge oil painting falling from the sky, and it was like a colorful halo on the head of Buddha. Sunrise is an important symbol of Mount Tai. It has always been cherished by tourists, and it is also written by many ancient literati.

Best viewing time: autumn

The best place to watch: Daiding Gongbei Stone and Yuhuanding Riguanfeng

The Jade Plate of the Sea of Clouds

In summer, when a large amount of water vapor evaporates after rain in Chu Qing, and the generated cloud and mist are controlled at approximately the same height, if tourists climb Mount Tai on foot, pass through the Zhongtianmen Gate, enter and cut Yun Jian and Yunbu Bridge, they will feel like entering a fairyland, walking in clouds and driving in fog. When they reach the top of the Jade Emperor, overlooking the sea of clouds below, they will feel like a rushing ocean all around. The low mountains have been submerged by the sea of clouds, and the peaks are exposed to the tops of clouds, like fairy islands in the sea. They will appear and disappear from time to time, and the travelers will be floating in the air

Best viewing time: summer

Best place to watch: Yuhuangding

Evening glow

When dusk is approaching, standing at the top of Mount Tai and looking to the west, one can appreciate the magnificent scenery of "Cangshan is like the sea, and the sun setting is like color". Gold streaks through clouds and mist, plunging into the world. Under the backdrop of the sunset, Mount Tai's numerous clouds and peaks are inlaid with a layer of golden bright edges, flashing with brilliance. If the sea of clouds appeared at this time and the sky was full of glowing rays, the scenery would be even more intoxicating. Everything seemed to be burning between the mountains and rivers.

The best viewing time: spring, summer and autumn

Best place to watch: Yuhuangding

Yellow River Gold Belt

Every time when the sun is setting, a golden ribbon will shine on the northwest side of Mount Tai. Its shape is wave, light, scaly, silvery, yellow and white, stretching from southwest to northeast to the junction of heaven and earth. The Qing Dynasty poet Yuan Mei described this golden belt of the Yellow River vividly in "Poems on Mount Tai": "A belt of yellow water is like a garment that pierces the world and connects with the Milky Way."

Best viewing time: autumn

Best place to watch: Yuhuangding

Foggy branches

Foggy branches are the magnificent scenery of Mount Tai in the winter half year. Fog is like frost but not frost, like ice but not ice. It is full of mountains and various shapes, turning rocks into "uncut jade", deadwood into "coral" and shrubs full of "flowers". Once the sun shines, the whole Mount Tai will be glittering and translucent, extremely pure and beautiful. Wusong hanging branches are also praised as "Qiongzhi in Yushu" and "Pear Flowers First Released", which is a unique scenic spot of Mount Tai.

Best viewing time: winter

Mount Tai Stone Carvings

Taishan stone carvings contain the history of Chinese calligraphy and show the development of Chinese calligraphy art in a unique and consistent way. There are more than 30 kinds of stone carvings, most of which are natural stone carvings and a few are steles. They have extremely high ornamental value and historical value. Stone carvings of many great men and celebrities are now scattered all over Mount Tai, such as Mao Zedong's poetry, Guo Moruo's stone carvings, Xu Qianqian's stone carvings, and Qin Mount Tai's stone carvings, which were the earliest in history and were born in the Qin Dynasty.

scenic spot map

Mount Tai has eight scenic spots, mainly in the five core scenic spots: Red Gate, Central Tianshan, Southern Tianmen, Taohuayu and Houshiwu.

Red Gate Scenic Area

The Red Gate is the first gate of Mount Tai's three gates of heaven and is the oldest scenic spot in Mount Tai. In ancient times, it was the emperor's way to climb mountains. Along the way, the natural landscape is magnificent and beautiful, with numerous historical sites. The scenic spots are mostly the essence of Mount Tai, such as Daizongfang Arch, Tianmen, Confucius Landing Place, Hongmen Palace, Wanxian Building, San Guan Temple, Doumu Palace, Yuan Jun Temple, etc. Ancient emperors and generals climbed Mount Tai mostly from the middle road of Hongmen Scenic Area. Therefore, the middle road is known as the "Mount Tian Scenic Area" and is also called the "secluded area" of Mount Tai because of its deep and secluded scenery.

Zhongtianmen scenic spot

Zhongtianmen scenic spot is the only way to get up and down the mountain. Zhongtianmen was founded in the Qing Dynasty and is a two-column single-door stone square. In the east, there is Zhongxi Mountain, which is abrupt and beautiful. It can watch the sunrise and the sunset glow. In the west, Phoenix Ridge winds and stretches, overlooking its majestic appearance and the overall view of the city. The geological features of Zhongtianmen Fault, Heilongtan Waterfall and Dazhongqiao Rock Mass in Zhongtianmen Scenic Spot are in harmony with the humanistic landscapes of Sanyangguan, Wuxian Temple, Zhongtianmen and Yunbuqiao, which constitute a scenic spot with profound geological relics and profound historical and cultural accumulation.

Nantianmen scenic spot

Nantianmen scenic spot is also known as the three gates of heaven. The landmark building Nantianmen was built in 1264. It was rebuilt many times in Ming and Qing dynasties, decorated with red walls and covered with yellow glazed tiles. Looking up from below, it is like the imperial palace in the sky. It is the gateway to the top of Mount Tai. The entire Nantianmen scenic spot has many famous geological relics, such as Xianrenqiao, structural joints, polar top stone, Gongbei stone, etc. You can also visit scenic spots such as Tianjie, which is the end point of Mount Tai cableway. You can visit various Taoist buildings and temples of wealth in Tianjie. You can also stop to buy souvenirs and taste mountain delicacies.

Taohuayu scenic spot

Passing through Nantianmen scenic spot is Taohuayu scenic spot. Taohuayu is a valley near the north-west direction, formed by geological faults. It is full of strange peaks, thousands of pools and waterfalls, and lush bamboo forests. It is very interesting to see the "male" of Mount Tai. In Taohuayu scenic spot, you can see Cuiping Mountain, Bijia Mountain, Huangshi Cliff and Yixiantian, as well as Longwan, Diaoyutai, Yixianquan and Caishixi. Now, sightseeing cableways have been set up to reach Daiding directly from the scenic spot. In addition, there is also a tourist center, which integrates catering, medical aid, tourist reception, information display, tourism consultation and other functions.

Houshiwu Scenic Area

Houshiwu scenic spot has been called Dai yin's first cave since ancient times. The scenic area is characterized by shihe, shihai, Tianzhufeng and Cangsong ancient cypress. geological relics and natural landscape are perfectly combined. Here, the rocks are jagged, peaks and valleys are deep, and the vast expanse of land is secluded. The breeze is blowing and the waves are blowing and the air is flying. The farther you go, the more dangerous the road is. The more strange the mountain is, the better the scenery is. Fanshi Town, Shihai Shihe River, Dazhigou Waterfall, Mingtang Ruins, yuquan temple and outcrops of Shangzhuang Ductile Shear Zone are all worth watching.

mountaineering route

Mount Tai Scenic Area has four entrances: Red Gate, Outer Village Gate, Tianzhufeng Gate and Taohuayu Gate. Enter through 4 entrances and there are mainly 4 mountaineering routes. Generally 90% of the people choose to climb the mountain from Hongmen and Waicunmen.

(Daimiao-Daizongfang Arch) Hongmen Palace-Wanxian Building (entrance ticket office)-Doumu Palace-stone valley-Hutiange-Huimaling-Zhongtianmen-Happy Three Miles-Yunbuqiao-Wudaofusong-Eighteen Dishes-Nantianmen-Tianjie-Bixia Temple-Tangmoya-Wuyue alone-Yuhuangding-Gongbei Stone

The tour line of Mount Tai Middle Road is the most concentrated route of human and natural landscape, is the essence of Mount Tai, and is also the mountain climbing way of ancient emperors. travel equipment

Jacket/sweater/down jacket, raincoat/anti-skid waterproof boots, mountaineering staff, lighting equipment, sunscreen ...

Dressing guidelines

The top of Mount Tai must be equipped with warm clothing all the year round, with coats in spring and summer and down jackets in autumn and winter. In general, the temperature at the top of the mountain is 3-6℃ lower than that at the bottom of the mountain, even lower. Although the difference is not big during the day, it is obvious at night. Therefore, if you choose to climb the mountain at night, you must carry thick clothes, but there are military overcoats on the top of the mountain for rent.

The rainy season in Mount Tai is from mid-June to August. There are many rainy days. Take raincoats and non-slip waterproof boots with you.

Matters needing attention

(1) mountaineering is a physical activity, as far as possible with less equipment, necessities. Food should also be taken less, and it is not expensive to buy it on the mountain.

(2) on the way to zhongtianmen rent coat, but suggest to rent to the worse, not too burdensome. The coat is about 10-one for 15 yuan.

(3) It is suggested to start from the Red Gate Line when climbing Mount Tai for the first time.

(4) climbing at night, one must keep warm and the other must have lighting equipment. At night, there are also many people rushing to the top of the Jade Emperor to occupy the position. It is better to start climbing from Daizongfang Arch to be more convenient.

⑤ There is much snow on mountain climbing in winter, and some buses will be shut down. There are two roads from Zhongtianmen down the mountain, one is the classic trail down to Hongmen and the other is the road to Waitian Village.

⑥ Don't exercise too hard up and down the mountain to avoid straining muscles. Don't sit in the damp or tuyere during the rest, and don't take off your clothes and hat to avoid catching cold.

⑦ Massage the muscles of the waist and legs on your own during each rest on the way to prevent muscle stiffness.

accommodation

The choice of accommodation in and around the scenic spot is very rich. There are ordinary hotels, guest houses, youth hostels, inns, hostels and star hotels. The price ranges from 20 yuan to 8000 yuan per night. Except for winter, the price of accommodation in Mount Tai fluctuates slightly throughout the year, with the off-season accommodation price generally falling by 10%-15%. It is worth noting that most accommodations in Mount Tai are divided into weekend and non-weekend prices, with weekend prices generally rising by about 15%.

Internal accommodation in scenic spots

It is mainly concentrated in Nantianmen scenic spot, with more accommodation in Tianjie and Zhongtianmen. Visitors who want to watch the sunrise had better live in the scenic spot. They don't have to get up too early to climb the mountain the next day, which saves time and effort than living at the foot of the mountain.

External accommodation of scenic spots

It is recommended to live at the entrance to the red gate and the entrance to the outer village gate. First, it is close to the scenic spot; second, it is close to the urban area; third, the distance between the two entrances is also close.
