Lijiang Ancient City Scenic Area in Yunnan
World heritage 5A scenic spot
Lijiang ancient city in the ancient city district of Lijiang, Yunnan is one of China's four largest ancient cities. The national AAAAA scenic spot is one of the ancient cities in China that successfully declared world cultural heritage with the entire ancient city. Lijiang ancient city is an ancient city without city walls. It is mainly composed of ancient streets, bridges, wooden houses and various ancient buildings. There are smooth and clean green stone roads, hand-built civil structure houses, small bridges and flowing water, colorful local ethnic customs and recreational activities, unique Naxi ancient music, Dongba ceremony, divination culture, ancient town bars, Naxi torch festival, etc. The following is an introduction to Lijiang's ancient city tourism strategy from scenic spots, self-help tour routes, surrounding scenic spots, surrounding self-driving tours.

scenic spot introduction

Historical culture

The old town of Lijiang was built in the late song dynasty and early yuan dynasty (late 13th century). the wooden ancestors of Lijiang moved their ruling center from the old town of Baisha to the present lion rock and began to build houses and cities, which are called "big leaf fields". The origin of the name of the ancient city is said to be because the hereditary ruler of Lijiang is named Mu, and the building of the city is bound to be like a wooden character framed with the word "trapped".

Lijiang ancient city is the gathering place of Naxi nationality. Its folk culture is mainly composed of Naxi nationality culture, but influenced by the "change of soil and return to flow", its cultural style is synchronized with the mainland.

Famous Figures in Lijiang Ancient City: Zhou Lin and Fang Guoyu

Geographical environment

Lijiang, Yunnan, is located in the transition zone between the Qinghai-Tibet Plateau and the Yunnan-Guizhou Plateau. The ancient city of Lijiang is about 2400 meters above sea level. Normal people usually do not have altitude sickness in Lijiang ancient city. Lijiang has a low latitude plateau monsoon climate with plenty of sunshine, strong ultraviolet rays, dry winter, strong wind, cool summer and plenty of rain. The annual temperature difference in Lijiang is small, and the daily temperature difference in the morning and evening is large. The lowest temperature in winter is about 0℃ and the highest temperature in summer is about 26℃.

The climate of Lijiang ancient city is influenced by Gao Yuanfeng in South Asia. Its climate has obvious vertical distribution, distinct dry and wet seasons and little temperature change. Although it is located on a plateau and sees snow-capped mountains all the year round, it has abundant rainfall and distinct dry and wet seasons, and belongs to the southwest monsoon climate. The Lijiang Dam, where the ancient city of Lijiang is located, has 2530 hours of sunshine and sufficient light energy. The annual solar radiation is 146.5 kilocalories per square centimeter, which is the highest value area in Yunnan Province.

featured landscape

Ancient street

The streets of Lijiang's ancient city are built on the mountain and run along the current. They are paved with red breccia (five-flowered stone). The rainy season is not muddy and the dry season is not flyash. The patterns on the stone are natural and elegant, with fine texture, which complement the whole city environment.

Famous scenic spots: Sifang Street, Guangyi Street, Qiyi Street, Wuyi Street and Xinhua Street.

Ancient bridge

There are 354 bridges on the yuhe river system in Lijiang ancient city area, with an average density of 93 bridges per square kilometer. Forms include covered bridges (wind and rain bridges), stone arch bridges, stone slab bridges, wooden bridges, etc.

Famous scenic spots: Suocui Bridge, Dashiqiao Bridge, Wanqian Bridge, Nanmen Bridge, Saddle Bridge and Renshou Bridge

Ancient architecture

The ancient city of Lijiang, as a city of ethnic minorities, combines the essence of the Han, Bai, Yi and Tibetan nationalities from the overall layout of the city to the engineering and architecture. It has its own unique style of Naxi nationality.

Famous scenic spots: Mufu, Wangu Building, Lijiang Ancient City Waterwheel and other scenic spots.

Square street

Sifang Street is a landmark and snack street of Lijiang ancient city. It is located in the core of the ancient city and is the commercial gathering place of the ancient city. There are many shops, restaurants and bars on the street, which are full of tourists. In addition, Sifang Street is also an important place for the residents of the ancient city to celebrate festivals. This is the place where they hold bonfire parties and tourists here can join in.

Wooden mansion

Mufu can be called the "grand view garden" of Lijiang ancient city culture. it is the palace of Lijiang chieftain of past dynasties. the TV series "mufu fengyun", which tells the story of Lijiang chieftain, was filmed here. The most distinctive feature of Mufu is its unique architectural style, "built in imitation of the Forbidden City", which is a combination of the Central Plains architectural style of the Tang, Song and Ming dynasties and Naxi traditional architectural culture.

Big water wheel

The large waterwheel is a landmark building at the main entrance to the north of Lijiang ancient city. It is also a symbol of Lijiang ancient city. It is the place to take photos when visiting. In addition, the Dongba cultural wall beside it is a full-bodied Naxi amorous feelings painting, which is called the Qingming Riverside Painting in Lijiang, and is also a place worth appreciating and taking photos.

Dashiqiao

Dashiqiao is the head of the ancient bridge in the ancient city. It has a history of more than 600 years and is one of the landmarks of the ancient city. The stone bridge covered with plant flowers is very suitable for taking photos. In addition, there are many snack bars near Dashiqiao, and shops with various characteristics are also very dense, so it is also very good to stroll around.

Sierra Leone

Lion Rock Ecological Park is the best place to watch the panorama of the ancient city. On its east side, there is a viewing platform surrounded by white and Chinese jade balustrades, which is a good place to overlook the ancient city of Lijiang. There is also the Wangu Building, one of the landmark buildings of the ancient city, on the mountain. The Wangu Building is not only beautiful but also the tallest building in Lijiang. The fifth floor of the Wangu Building is a good place to have a panoramic view of the ancient city.

Naxi Xi Hospital

Naxi Xi Yuan is a concentrated display of Naxi marriage customs culture, showing the whole process of Naxi marriage. You can go to learn about Naxi people's alternative wedding customs and wedding decoration styles.

To beat a copper family

Located at 76 wenzhi lane, Wuyi street, the ancient city, da tong shijia is hammered by the son of a skilled Naxi copper craftsman family and the elder jikuan. here, a dazzling array of Lijiang copper products and the traditional copper making process are displayed. Lijiang bronze wares can be bought if you like because of their good color, sufficient weight, many varieties and durability.

Bar Street

The ancient city bar street is a paradise for bar owners. here, all kinds of famous wine reels are waving with the wind. drums, keyboards, guitars, bass and so on are playing music together. Here, the ancient city is integrated with bars, history and modernity. A glass of beer with antique wooden chairs and rattan chairs has no sense of discord. Lijiang Bar Street is the most characteristic roadway in the ancient city, attracting guests from all over the world. < /p>

Scenic weather

The climate of Lijiang ancient city is influenced by Gao Yuanfeng in South Asia. Its climate has obvious vertical distribution, distinct dry and wet seasons and little temperature change. Although it is located on a plateau and sees snow-capped mountains all the year round, there is plenty of rainfall.

Spring weather in Lijiang (March-May): the temperature is about 5℃-20℃, mainly sunny days; Summer weather in Lijiang (June-August): the temperature is about 10℃-26℃, rainy, cooler in the morning and evening, hotter in the daytime and cooler in the indoor. July and August are rainy seasons and often rain. Autumn weather in Lijiang (September-November): the temperature is about 5℃-23℃, one third in rainy days and one third in sunny days and cloudy days. Winter weather in Lijiang (December-February): the temperature is about 0℃-15℃, the temperature is relatively low, the air is dry, and it is sunny in most cases.
