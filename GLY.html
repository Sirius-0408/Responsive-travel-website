Xiamen Gulangyu Scenic Area
World heritage 5A scenic spot
Gulangyu Scenic Area is a national 5A scenic spot, a national key cultural relic protection unit, a list of world cultural heritage, a national scenic spot and a national civilized scenic spot. Gulangyu Island is the largest island in Xiamen with many Chinese and western buildings, short streets and criss-crossing streets. Gulangyu Island is known as a "sea garden". The island has a pleasant climate, no traffic noise, and full of birds and flowers. It has always been said that "it is useless to travel to Xiamen without visiting Gulangyu Island". The following is a brief introduction to Gulangyu's tourism strategy from Gulangyu's scenic spots, self-help tour routes, scenic spot distribution maps, surrounding scenic spots, surrounding self-driving tours.

Scenic spot introduction

Historical culture

Gulangyu Island was called "round sandbar" in Song Dynasty and Yuan Dynasty. It was not until Ming Dynasty that it was renamed Gulangyu Island. At the end of the Ming Dynasty, Zheng Chenggong still stationed troops in Gulangyu to train his navy division. Gulangyu Island was also established in the Qing Dynasty as one of the five largest Australian cities in Xiamen and gradually prospered. In November 1843, it was reduced to a concession and 13 countries such as Germany, the United States, France and Japan set up consulates on the island. It didn't return until October 17 after 1949.

①Geographical position

Gulangyu Island is located in the southwest corner of Xiamen Peninsula. It faces Xiamen Island across the sea and is adjacent to Haicang District and Siming District of Xiamen.

②Geological landform

Gulangyu Island, as an island, has formed a variety of sea erosion landforms with various features such as sea cliffs, sea erosion platforms, sea erosion pillars and sea caves.

③physioclimate

There is more rain in spring, typhoons in summer, less rain and more sunshine in autumn, cold and chilling in winter, and sea and land winds are extremely obvious. Sea fog occurs from November to June of the following year, with March being the most.

④natural resource

The coverage rate of green space is over 40%, with a total of more than 90 families and 1,000 species of plants. Magnolia grandiflora, Cinnamomum camphora, Nanyangshan, Phoenix Tree and Putao, which are common in subtropical areas, are all planted in front of and behind houses or on the roadside.

Traffic inside scenic spots

Because Gulangyu's streets are not spacious and the scenic spots are close to each other, it is mainly on foot and can be transported by battery cars. Don't worry about getting lost, there will be signs along the way and maps on the package.

The price of the battery car is 50 yuan/person, and the operation time is 7: 00-19: 30 (May 1-September 30); From 7: 00 to 18: 00 (October 1 to April 30 of the following year).

Featured landscape

Centennial architecture

Gulangyu was once the concession of 13 western countries, leaving behind a large number of western-style buildings, accounting for 90% of all the buildings in Gulangyu, such as villas, mansions, consulates, church schools, churches, etc. These buildings were later renovated and renovated. For example, Lin Heshou's "Bagua Building" was changed into Gulangyu Organ Museum, which was originally a gigantic "White House" building. There are also the landmark consulates of various countries, the Golden Melon Building, the Daffodil, etc. During the tour, it was as if it had passed back a hundred years ago.

Haoyueyuan

This is a garden with beaches, rocks, green trees and pavilions. There is a 13.7-meter-long, 4.7-meter-high, 18-ton copper-consuming Zheng Chenggong and his department's huge bronze statue. Apart from Zheng Chenggong and his department, this huge bronze sculpture is slightly larger than the real one, and the rest of the military forces extend to both sides. The picture is very wide and attractive. However, what is more prominent is a giant granite statue of Zheng Chenggong, 15.7 meters high and weighing 1,617 tons. It is carved out of 625 pieces of "Quanzhou White" granite on 23 floors and is very powerful.

Piano museum

The museum displays more than 40 clavichords collected by patriotic overseas Chinese Hu Youyi. Among them are rare and precious gold-plated pianos, the world's earlier four-cornered pianos and the larger upright piano, the ancient hand-cranked pianos, the foot-operated automatic piano made 100 years ago and the eight-foot-operated clavichord. Its value cannot be estimated. French piano master Michel Burdun Koehler and our famous conductor Han Zhongjie and others have all specially visited here.

International Art Museum of Engraving

An engraving art museum in China is also a large-scale and high-grade international engraving art museum in the world. The first floor is the international exhibition hall and the second floor is the China exhibition hall. The museum has 407 pieces of works from five countries including China, Japan, South Korea, Singapore and Malaysia, representing the high artistic standard of lettering at home and abroad.

The Palace Museum Gulangyu Foreign Heritage Museum

It was jointly built by Xiamen municipal government and the national palace museum. it is the first thematic branch set up locally by the national palace museum and focuses on displaying foreign cultural relics collected by the national palace museum from the Ming and Qing dynasties to the present. At present, 245 pieces (sets) of cultural relics on display are completely preserved and come from France, Germany, Switzerland, Russia, Czech Republic, India, Turkey, etc.

Map of scenic spots

Sunlight Rock

As the saying goes, "it does not count as Xiamen if you don't go to Riguangyan". Riguangyan is the leading scenic spot on Gulangyu Island, including Riguangyan and Qinyuan. There is a huge rock with a diameter of more than 40 meters in the sky on the summit of Sunlight Rock, which is a symbol of Xiamen. The scenic spot is full of subtropical romance, with a tile, heron river dragon cave, ancient summer cave, water platform and other scenic spots. Climbing to the top of the pole, you can overlook Gulangyu Island and the surrounding islands. Entering the Qin Garden, you can watch Bainiao Garden, Hero Garden and so on, which form an interesting contrast with Sunlight Rock.

Xiamen undersea world

Here is a specimen of the world's largest sperm whale captured from Xiamen waters. In addition, it has more than 350 species of freshwater fishes from all over the world and more than 10,000. The undersea tunnel is 80 meters long and 1.5 meters wide. When entering the undersea tunnel, standing on the electric walking path, you can see the concave fish ponds on both sides and the world of fish in all directions. Fish schools are close at hand.

Fishbone museum of art

There is a huge shark bone in the museum, which is the treasure of the town hall. In addition, all the paintings on display are made of natural fish bones one by one.

Hong Kong Tsai Hou Beach

The velocity of sea water is small after Hong Kong Tsai. The water temperature in the sea is above 20℃ for five and a half months every year. The swimming season lasts for half a year. There are tides twice a day. The swimming time is more than six hours. It is a natural bathing beach. There are also yachts and motorboats. He once held the National OP Class Sailing Championship here.

Shuzhuang Garden

Formerly a private garden for a wealthy Taiwanese businessman, Lin Erjia. Shuzhuang Garden leans against rocks and faces the sea. It is adjacent to Guanhai Garden in the east and behind Gangzai in the west. The whole garden is divided into two parts: Tibetan Sea Garden and Bushan Garden, each with five scenes. The five sights of the Tibetan sea are Meishou Hall, Renqiu Pavilion, Zhendu Pavilion, 44th Bridge and Zhaoting Pavilion. The five scenic spots of bushan are the stone mountain house, the twelve caves, also love my house, the listening tower and the Xiao LAN pavilion.

Sea paradise structure

One of Gulangyu's ten largest villas, Haitangtiangu Old Villa is a model of the combination of Chinese and western architectural cultures. It is a villa complex symmetrically arranged along the central axis. There are five villas in total, with the most distinctive one. Currently, three villas are open to the public. No.34 has been developed into Nanyang Cafe, No.42 is the performing arts center, and No.38 is the main building as the art museum.

The Lin Yutang House

This U-shaped villa is one of the ancient villas on Gulangyu Island. It was once the residence of Gulangyu's richest man, Liao Jia, for more than 150 years. The villa is English, with two rooms and one hall in the front. In the middle of the back is a small flower bed, with two-storey houses on both sides, connecting the main house in front and a fish pond in the back garden. The stone steps on the first floor are surrounded by ancient banyan, longan and magnolia trees, which shade the small garden with elegance and warmth.

Dressing guidelines

The climate in spring and autumn is the most pleasant. T-shirts and coats are enough. Short sleeve shorts in summer but pay attention to sun protection. Winter has the lowest temperature in January, so thick down jackets are necessary. You can put on shirts and knitwear inside. Cotton jackets may not be as warm as down jackets.

Matters needing attention

(1) Wear a life jacket when taking the ferry round trip. Do not tie loose knots on the strap, which is easy to fall off. < strong > the lifejacket has a whistle for survival.

(2) In the lobby of the tourist center about 100m from the dock, a luggage storage office will be set up to provide goods storage service for tourists.

(3) The island is mainly visited by walking. It will be more comfortable to prepare a pair of sports casual shoes.

(4) Black mosquitoes appear in spring, summer and autumn on the island, especially in July. If you are bitten, your skin will become red, swollen, itchy and painful, and you can go to Xiamen No.1 Hospital on the island for treatment.

(5) to Xiamen to eat seafood must remember to take antidiarrheal, not greedy.

(6) Xiamen is a national health city. The local government imposes heavier penalties for littering and destroying the cleanliness of the city. Smoking is prohibited in public places.
