Wulingyuan Scenic and Historic Interest Area of Zhangjiajie
World heritage World geology
Wulingyuan Scenic Area is located in the hinterland of Wuling Mountains in northwest Hunan Province, with a total area of 397 square kilometers. Wulingyuan has China's first national forest park, which is a world natural heritage, a world geological park and the country's first 5A scenic spot. Wulingyuan has a complex geological structure and unique landscape. It is known as "Qifeng 3000 and Xiushui 800". The US movie Avatar and the Chinese classic dramas A Dream of Red Mansions, Journey to the West, Zhong Kui's Demon and monster hunt were filmed here. scenic spot introduction

historical culture

Wulingyuan used to be a border area. At that time, traffic was blocked, boats and cars were isolated, and people were rarely seen by outsiders. During the Republic of China, it first belonged to Wuling Road, and then to the second, fourth and eighth administrative supervision districts of Hunan Province. Since ancient times, many scholars have mentioned Wulingyuan in their works. Wang Wei wrote "living on the uplands, above the Wuling River, on farms and in gardens that are like a world apart"; Li Bai also wrote "when the achievements have been brushed away, they will be relegated to Wulingyuan".

Geographical environment

geographic location

Wulingyuan, located in the northwest of Hunan Province, consists of Zhangjiajie National Forest Park, Suoxiyu Nature Reserve in Cili County and Tianzishan Nature Reserve in Sangzhi County, with a total area of about 500 square kilometers.

geology and geomorphology

It covers six types of landform: Zhangjiajie landform, Shi Ying sandstone, structural dissolution landform, denudation structural landform, valley landform and geological relics. The geological remains originated from the Devonian period and are ancient environments hundreds of millions of years ago.

natural climate

It belongs to the subtropical mountain prototype monsoon humid climate. In a year, April-July is a relatively wet season and August-September is a relatively dry season. Snowfall is mainly concentrated from December to February of the following year.

natural resources

The forest coverage rate reached 85.29%, 1,630 species of vascular plants and nearly 100 species of national and provincial protected animals.

featured landscape

Sandstone Peak Forest and Cloud Sea in Shi Ying

Sandstone Peak Forest in Shi Ying is a rare geomorphic type at home and abroad, and only Wulingyuan is the most famous, which has research value. At present, there are more than 3,000 peaks known. When the rain turns clear or the rain lasts for a long time, clouds and smoke will appear in the faint valleys. Clouds and mist will float in the mountainous areas. The sea of clouds will be thick and light, while the stone peaks will appear and disappear, and the scene will change a lot. Therefore, this is often where photographers stop.

Best Scenic Spot: Tianzi Mountain

Best viewing time: summer

Canyon landscape

People have always praised the waterfalls, springs, streams, pools and lakes in Wulingyuan scenic spot with "800 beautiful waters". What cannot be missed when you come to Wulingyuan is Jin Bianxi in the National Forest Park. The Pipa River in the west and Suoxi in the east of Jin Bianxi is a winding and deep canyon. It is the most beautiful border of Wulingyuan Scenic Area. The scenery on both sides is beautiful and there are too many things to see. Among them, Golden Whip Rock, Eagle Guard Whip, Wenxingyan, Shikonin Pool, Meeting over a thousand miles and Jumping Fish Pool are the most peculiar. The general tour time is about 2.5 to 3 hours.

Best Scenic Spots: Along Jin Bianxi

Best viewing time: spring

Cave landscape

Wulingyuan has a large number of caves, large scale and very special features. The famous ones include Huanglong Cave, Guanyin Cave, Xiangshui Cave, Guiqi Cave and Feiyun Cave, among which the famous one is Suoxiyu Huanglong Cave. The Huanglong cave is 7.5 kilometers long and is divided into four layers, including a reservoir, two rivers, three hanging waterfalls, four pools, 13 halls and 96 corridors. The essence of Huanglong Cave lies in the "ice bell", "green bamboo passageway" and "dragon palace dancing".

Best Sight Spots: Inside Huanglong Cave

The best viewing time: spring, summer and autumn

Lake landscape

The main lake landscape in the scenic spot is Baofeng Lake. Baofeng Lake is an artificial lake built by river closure and damming. It is 72 meters deep and 2.5 meters long. It is also a rare high gorge Pinghu with green hills, clear water and charming scenery.

The best viewing time: spring and summer

Rime

Every winter, there are rime and ice hanging landscapes in the high-rise areas such as Tianzi Mountain, Huangshizhai, Yuanjiajie and Yang Jiajie in Wulingyuan. After the branches and leaves on the top of the mountain are covered with a thick layer of ice and snow, they are exquisitely carved, jade clean and ice clear, like fairy tale world, which makes people intoxicated. The best places to watch rime are Tianzi Mountain, Tianzi Pavilion, Yunqingyan, etc.

Best viewing time: winter

Pastoral scenery

Wulingyuan is a settlement of Tujia, Bai, Miao and other ethnic minorities, terraced fields and houses dotted with green hills and waters. The green trees and smoke curl up from kitchen chimneys. If you are lucky enough to catch up with local festivals, you can also enjoy ethnic songs and dances, eat baked glutinous rice cakes stuffed with white sugar or honey, and see the Diaojiao Building built on the mountain and mysterious magic.

The best viewing time: spring, summer and autumn < /p>

Zhangjiajie National Forest Park

With a total area of 130 square kilometers, it was included in the World Natural Heritage List by the United Nations in 1992 and in the World Geopark in 2004. The park can be divided into six small scenic spots, including Huangshizhai, Pipaxi, Jin Bianxi, Yaozizhai, Shedaogou and Yuanjiajie. Among them, Huangshizhai is the most famous, known as "if you don't reach Huangshizhai, you'll never reach Zhangjiajie". Huang Shizhai has countless stone peaks and pillars, rugged and straight, densely distributed, forming a vast peak forest. If you encounter a continuous sea of clouds, peaks and white gauze shawls will change rapidly. The Tianshu Treasure Box on the Peak, the Nantian Pillar and the natural frescoes are all worth visiting.

Best time to play: spring.

Suoxiyu scenic spot

The total area is about 180 square kilometers. There are many scenic spots in the area, which are scattered all over the place. "Shan Qi, Shuixiu, Bridge Risks, and Deep Cave" are its biggest characteristics. There are mainly Baofeng Lake, Huanglong Cave, Baizhang Gorge, Shili Gallery, Xihai Peak Forest and other landscapes. Here is not only a grotesque cave, the gorge and lake depend on the sky, there are many birds and animals, there are all kinds of plants, but also the landscape is unique, which can be viewed in succession or divided into gardens. It can be said that there is a landscape in the landscape and a garden outside the garden.

Best time to play: spring.

tianzishan scene

With a total area of 67 square kilometers, it is named after the ancient Tujia leader Xiang Dakun who claimed to be the emperor. At present, Tianzi Mountain scenic spot is in the prime of its peak forest evolution history. It is known as the "king of peak forest" with numerous stone peaks and ravines on the east, west and south. The territory includes six small scenic spots, including the stone eaves, the collapsed tea tray, the old house market, yuanyang creek, huanglongquan and fengqi mountain. Among them, the scenery on the eaves of the stone house is relatively concentrated, which is called "looking at the sky through the railing". The main landscapes include Helong Park, Yubi Peak, fairy flowers, warriors training horses, etc.

The best time to play: spring and autumn.

Yang Jiajie scenic spot

The total area is about 34 square kilometers. The names of places and mountains in Yang Jiajie are very distinctive. The names of Zongbaofeng, Liulang Bay and Yang Jiajie are taken by the descendants of Yang Jiajiang living here in memory of Yang Jiajiang. They have remained unchanged for more than 1,000 years. At present, the scenic spots in Yang Jiajie are in their infancy in the history of peak forest evolution, with ravines, clear streams, dense vegetation and forest coverage as high as 95%. Yang Jiajie is the last of the four scenic spots in Wulingyuan. The territory includes Xiangzhi Creek and Qingfeng Gorge. Among them, Xiangzhi Creek is the most famous. Its main landscapes include one-step climbing into the sky, air corridor, Liulang Peak, etc.

Best play time: April-October. < /p>

practical information

Scenic weather

real-time weather inquiry > >

The temperature difference between day and night in Zhangjiajie is large, and the temperature difference in the four seasons is relatively small. The temperature difference between Zhangjiajie's core scenic spot and the urban area is about 5-10℃, the hottest time is 39℃ in the urban area, and the scenic spot is about 30℃.

April-June and September-November: The temperature on the mountain in spring and autumn is lower than that in the city. In early summer, you still need to wear long-sleeved clothes. At night, you also need to sleep with a thin quilt and keep warm. Especially in May, there is often a rainy season. Remember to bring an umbrella or raincoat.

July-August, December-February: It's cool on the mountain in summer. Pay attention to sun protection in the city. It snows on the mountain in winter. You can take a climbing stick, wear non-slip shoes and keep warm.

travel equipment

(1) bring band-aids, anti-inflammatory tablets, wet tissues, disposable raincoats, waterproof shoes, sunscreen products with an index of more than 30, mosquito bites, sunglasses, sun hats, etc ..

(2) The mountain road is rugged and the road is far away, so it is better to wear thick-soled non-slip sports shoes or hiking shoes, and to have more pairs of socks.

Matters needing attention

① mountain climbing takes a lot of physical strength. a little dry food and bottled water can be prepared. no picnic is allowed.

(2) wet mountain road in rainy days, rain and fog will lead to blurred vision, it is best not to risk climbing in the rain.

(3) Zhangjiajie scenic area has a luggage check-in service between the railway station and the accommodation point in the scenic area. luggage can be checked to the house to be checked in at the check-in point near the railway station. On the other hand, luggage can also be checked to the railway station in advance in the scenic spots, and then you can pick it up directly. Each piece of luggage is 10 yuan. It is suggested that before consigning, it is best to check with the owner of the accommodation to see if there is such business in their home.

④ the three large groups of monkeys in the scenic spot are "local strongmen". don't provoke them and don't expose your food to avoid being robbed by monkeys and hurting yourself at the same time.

⑤ Luggage can be sent to 2 yuan at ticket offices in most scenic spots and will not be lost in general.

⑥ Do not buy "Thousand-year-old Polygonum multiflorum" when shopping, they are all fake.

⑦ Don't believe what the so-called so-and-so travel agency staff say on the train. They usually sell it to the travel agency at the price of 100-150 yuan people, and the management is not perfect.

notes for photography tour

(1) Sunrise in summer and autumn is about 5: 30, sunset is 19: 00, spring and winter is about 6: 20, sunset is 18: 20.
